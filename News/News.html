Clarjon Law Professor and Regent, Justice Kyla, Denies Allegations of a Presidential Run

Putting recent political speculations to rest, Supreme Court Justice and esteemed Clarjon University professor Kyla categorically denied rumors of a planned presidential campaign. She reaffirmed her unwavering commitment to the judiciary and to mentoring the next generation of legal luminaries inside the Clarjon lecture halls.

During a press conference held at the University Executive Hall, Justice Kyla emphasized that her primary mandate remains the pursuit of truth and the cultivation of unparalleled legal minds. "My duty is to the law and to this institution. The future of our nation is shaped here, within these walls, not on the campaign trail," she stated.
