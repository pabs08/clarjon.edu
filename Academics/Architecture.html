Elite Degree Programs

Rigorous undergraduate programs designed to forge master builders and spatial visionaries.

Bachelor of Science in Architecture

Our flagship program that demands absolute mastery of structural integrity, aesthetic brilliance, and advanced Building Information Modeling. Graduates are primed to lead global architectural firms and construct iconic skyscrapers.

Bachelor of Science in Interior Design

Focuses on the extreme optimization and luxurious detailing of internal spaces. Scholars master human ergonomics, lighting architecture, and the psychology of spatial experience for elite commercial and residential developments.

Bachelor of Science in Landscape Architecture

Merging environmental science with structural design. This program trains scholars to engineer massive urban parks, sustainable ecological infrastructures, and climate-resilient global cities.

Bachelor of Science in Urban Planning and Development

The strategic mastery of metropolitan expansion. Scholars dictate zoning laws, transportation networks, and macro-economic spatial strategies to construct the smart megacities of the future.

The Clarjon Spatial Simulation Studio

Our scholars draft their masterworks inside a multi-million peso studio equipped with holographic projection tables, full-immersion Virtual Reality environments, and automated laser fabrication labs. This allows architectural blueprints to be explored and stress-tested in absolute reality before a single foundation is laid.
