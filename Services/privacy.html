Institutional Commitment to Privacy

Clarjon University recognizes the fundamental right to privacy of all its stakeholders, including scholars, faculty, alumni, and partners. This Privacy Policy outlines our uncompromising standards for collecting, using, and protecting data in compliance with global data protection regulations and the highest ethical benchmarks.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal and academic information necessary for official matriculation, institutional research, and the delivery of premium student services. This includes, but is not limited to, identity credentials, scholastic history, and financial records for scholarship administration.

2. Purpose of Processing

Data is utilized exclusively for academic governance, strategic university planning, and enhancing the scholar experience. We do not engage in the unauthorized distribution or commercialization of any personal data gathered within our systems.

3. Data Security Measures

The Information Technology division employs military-grade encryption and advanced cybersecurity protocols to safeguard all institutional repositories. Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to authorized executive personnel only.

4. Rights of the Individual

All stakeholders possess the right to access, verify, and request the rectification of their personal records. Requests for data portability or the permanent deletion of information must be formally submitted to the Director of Admissions and Registration for executive review.

5. Policy Updates

Clarjon University reserves the right to modify this policy as required by emerging global data laws or institutional advancements. Stakeholders are encouraged to review this document periodically to remain informed of our data governance standards.

For inquiries regarding data protection, please contact our Information Security Team.
